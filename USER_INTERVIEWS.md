# User Interviews

# Objective

The goal of these conversations was to understand how developers and startup teams currently manage spending on AI tools and whether they face issues with overlapping subscriptions or unclear costs.

---

# Interview 1 — Student Developer

## Background
A student using:
- ChatGPT Plus
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot

## Key Insights
- Did not track total monthly AI spending
- Subscribed to multiple tools for similar tasks
- Interested in cheaper alternatives
- Wanted a simple dashboard instead of spreadsheets

## Takeaway
Students and solo developers often underestimate how much they spend collectively on AI tools.

---

# Interview 2 — Startup Founder

## Background
A small startup founder managing AI subscriptions for a 5-person team.

## Key Insights
- Team members used different AI tools independently
- No centralized tracking system existed
- Unsure whether premium plans were fully utilized
- Interested in optimization recommendations

## Takeaway
Small teams quickly lose visibility into SaaS spending when AI adoption grows.

---

# Common Patterns Observed

Across both conversations, the most common problems were:

- Lack of visibility into total AI spending
- Multiple overlapping subscriptions
- Unused premium plans
- Difficulty comparing AI tools
- No simple optimization guidance

---

# Product Decisions Influenced by Interviews

These conversations influenced several product decisions:

## Public Shareable Reports
Users liked the idea of sharing optimization reports with teams or founders.

## Simple UI
Most users preferred a quick audit flow instead of complex dashboards.

## AI-Generated Recommendations
Users found structured optimization summaries more useful than raw pricing tables.

## Lead Capture
Adding optional company details helps identify startup teams with larger optimization opportunities.

---

# Conclusion

The interviews validated that AI subscription spending is becoming difficult to track, especially for startups and developers using multiple tools simultaneously.

AuditAI was designed to simplify this process by combining spend tracking, optimization suggestions, and shareable reports into one lightweight platform.
