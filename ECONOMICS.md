# Economics

# Core Business Idea

AuditAI helps startups and teams identify unnecessary spending on AI subscriptions and discover lower-cost alternatives through automated AI-powered audits.

The platform focuses on reducing wasted SaaS spending while generating qualified leads for future optimization services.

---

# Why This Problem Matters

AI tool adoption is growing rapidly, especially among startups and engineering teams.

Companies often subscribe to:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot
- Multiple AI APIs

Over time, teams:
- Overlap subscriptions
- Underuse expensive plans
- Pay for inactive seats
- Lose visibility into total AI spending

This creates a strong opportunity for spend optimization.

---

# Value Proposition

AuditAI provides:
- Quick spend visibility
- Optimization suggestions
- AI-generated recommendations
- Public shareable reports
- Lead qualification for high-saving accounts

The product reduces friction by generating results within minutes instead of requiring manual consulting.

---

# Cost Structure

Current operating costs are relatively low because the platform uses:
- Vercel free tier
- Render free tier
- MongoDB Atlas free tier
- Gemini API free/low-cost usage
- Resend free email tier

Main future costs would include:
- Higher AI API usage
- Database scaling
- Email infrastructure
- Analytics tooling
- Paid hosting upgrades

# Unit Economics

The platform has relatively low cost per audit because:
- Most processing is lightweight
- AI requests are prompt-based
- Public reports increase organic sharing
- Lead capture improves conversion opportunities

A single successful enterprise optimization lead could generate significantly more value than the infrastructure cost of hundreds of audits.
