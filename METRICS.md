# Metrics

# Key Product Metrics

The following metrics are important for measuring the performance, growth, and effectiveness of AuditAI.

---

# User Metrics

## Total Users
Tracks the number of registered users on the platform.

## Active Users
Measures how many users actively generate audits or revisit the platform.

## Returning Users
Tracks repeat usage and long-term engagement.

---

# Audit Metrics

## Total Audits Generated
Counts the number of completed AI spend audits.

## Average Audit Completion Rate
Measures how many users complete the full audit flow after starting.

## Average Estimated Savings
Tracks the average monthly and yearly savings identified by the platform.

## Most Audited AI Tools
Identifies which AI tools are most commonly analyzed.

Examples:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot

---

# Lead Metrics

## Lead Conversion Rate
Measures how many users submit lead information before viewing results.

## High-Savings Leads
Tracks users with large optimization opportunities who may become premium customers.

## Company Size Distribution
Tracks startup sizes using the platform.

---

# Sharing Metrics

## Public Report Views
Measures how many public audit links are opened.

## Share Rate
Tracks how often users share their audit reports.

## Viral Traffic
Measures traffic coming from shared report URLs.

---

# Product Performance Metrics

## API Response Time
Tracks backend and Gemini API response performance.

## Audit Generation Time
Measures how long it takes to generate a complete audit report.

## Error Rate
Tracks failed requests, validation issues, or API errors.

---

# Business Metrics

## Estimated Customer Savings
Total savings identified across all audits.

## Potential Revenue Opportunities
Tracks leads that may convert into premium optimization services.

## Cost Per Audit
Measures infrastructure and AI API costs required to generate one audit.

---

# Long-Term Metrics

Future metrics that could become important:
- Subscription retention
- Team collaboration usage
- Paid conversion rate
- Enterprise onboarding rate
- Vendor recommendation accuracy
- AI recommendation satisfaction score
