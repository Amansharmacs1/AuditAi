# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

# Target Audience

AuditAI is designed for:
- Early-stage startups
- Student founders
- Indie hackers
- Small development teams
- AI-heavy product teams

These users often subscribe to multiple AI tools without properly tracking overall spending.

---

# Problem Statement

Many teams are rapidly adopting AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, and GitHub Copilot, but most do not know:
- How much they are spending overall
- Which subscriptions are underutilized
- Where cheaper alternatives exist
- Whether multiple tools overlap in functionality

AuditAI solves this by generating quick AI-powered spend optimization reports.

---

# Positioning

AuditAI is positioned as:
> “An AI spend optimization assistant for startups and modern teams.”

The product focuses on simplicity, fast audits, and actionable recommendations instead of complicated financial dashboards.

---

# Acquisition Strategy

## 1. Viral Shareable Reports
Each audit generates a public shareable report URL.

This encourages:
- Founder-to-founder sharing
- Team discussions
- Social sharing
- Organic product discovery

---

## 2. LinkedIn & Twitter/X Content
Short-form content strategy:
- “How startups waste money on AI tools”
- “Best AI stack under $100/month”
- “5 hidden AI subscription costs”
- “Startup AI budget breakdowns”

These posts can drive traffic to free audits.

---

## 3. Startup Communities
Potential distribution channels:
- Product Hunt
- Reddit startup communities
- Indie Hackers
- LinkedIn founder groups
- College startup communities

---

## 4. Lead Capture Funnel
Before viewing results, users submit:
- Email
- Company name
- Role
- Team size

This creates a list of high-intent users interested in cost optimization.
